##### Overview

This course applies microeconomics and game theory to analyze firm decision-making, covering competitive advantage, entry, firm scope, and network effects. Format includes lectures, case discussions, and student presentations.

##### Cases

Moneyball, Cola Wars, Enterprise Rent-A-Car, Walmart vs. Amazon, Ritz-Carlton, Ryanair, NUMMI, McDonald's, Nintendo and Sega.

##### Details

+ **Level:** Undergraduate (Business Economics specialization)
+ **Institution:** University of Chicago
+ **Semesters:** Spring 2021, Spring 2022
